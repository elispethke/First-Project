About Me

I transitioned my career with over 20 years of experience as a saxophonist and music teacher, deciding to enter the field of technology. As a mobile app developer for iOS, with years of self-taught study, as well as formal education and certifications, I am passionate about creating user-friendly mobile applications. During this time, I have delved deeply into SwiftUI and SwiftUIKit. These endeavors showcase my proficiency in UI/UX design, app architecture, website development, and problem-solving. I am eager to contribute my expertise, gained through extensive study and hard work, to collaborative teams and continue my journey in creating innovative user-centric iOS applications. I am a communicative woman with exceptional teamwork skills, dedicated and motivated to learn. I am focused and determined in my personal and professional goals.
